Stir Fry Kangkong Recipe

The recipe for my favorite dish that I grew up with in my childhood.

Ingredients:

  • Kangkong (Water Spinach)
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chicken/Beef/Tofu

Step By Step:

  1. Cut up the leaves and stems of the Water Spinach.
  2. Dice up the garlic and cut the meat/tofu into bite sized pieces
  3. Heat up the pan with oil at low heat for 2 minutes and add in the diced-up garlic.
  4. Once garlic is cooked, add the meat and 3 tablespoons of soy sauce.
  5. Once meat is cooked, add in the water spinach and stir-fry.
  6. When the water spinach has wilted, put it in a plate and enjoy.
